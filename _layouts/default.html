I am a second-year PhD student at INRIA and Ecole Polytechnique, where I work on long-form video understanding. My PhD advisors are Ivan Laptev and Vicky Kalogeiton. Before my PhD, I graduated with a MS in Statistics from ENSAE Paris. During the summer of 2020, I completed an research internship at Capital Fund Management (CFM). From 2021 to 2023, I worked at OWKIN, a biotech startup, as a Data Scientist.
